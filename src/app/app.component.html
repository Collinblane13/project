Intro

Forming your body into something that you can be happy with is a hard thing to accomplish. Some people want to lose fat others are more conscerned with gaining muscle, this makes a dilemma where there is no one good way to accomplish everything That is what the site is made for! Your goals can be broken down and filtered through how many calories you need to ingest during the day, what workouts you need to do, and what specific food you are putting into your body.

About BMR

BMR is a fancy way of saying how many calories you body will naturally burn during the day. Sadly with how special everyone is, we can only give an estimate of where to start. With this estimate though you can better figure out your needs for your goals, and get to the point where you are balancing your calories in and calories out like a champ.

About Macros

Macros are probably the hardest part to actually commit to. This is the process of structing your diet in a way where so muc of a percentage of you calories comes from fat, another percentage comes from carbs, and the remaining percentage comes from protein.

About Workouts

Workouts are not jsut ways to burn calories so you can eat more during the day, they are also the best way to form the body that you want. Making choices between cardio and weight lifting, intense short exersices or longer more sustained endurance workouts, they help make your body function in specific ways.

How should you Balance Your Diet?

Carbs 45%-65%

Carbs are where you are going to get most of your quick energy from during the day Your body processes it quickly and helps give oyu energy for long workouts. It also can help you feel more full during a meal and aids with regualr bowel movements if you are eating the more beneficial whole-grain carbs.

Protein 10%-35%

Proteins are what most of you body gets made up of. This aids in the building of cells like hair, nails, etc, but also helps with the rebuilding of muscles after hard workouts. This rebulding helps with increasing strength gains and mass. You typically want to eat learner protien like chicken and fish.

Fat 20%-35%

Fat gets a bad reputaiton and most see it as only a bad thing, but fat is needed for you body to function fat is great for cell membrane health, that is why Omega-6 pills are so beneficial to people. Your body also uses fat to store energy for times when you do not have access to food, and insulation to protect your organs. Good fats are oils like coconut and olive oil and fat from fish.
